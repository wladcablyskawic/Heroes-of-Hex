SIMPLE LINE OF SIGHT
Line of Sight
is defined as model's
front arc, limited by possible
obstructions like hills or buildings. Unless otherwise
mentioned (War
Machines, Grey Seer on Screaming Bell).
Terrain pieces


Block LoS: Hills
and impassable terrain.

Don't block LoS: all other terrain.

blocking


Majority of unit's front rank is defending an obstacle, as
described on page 122
BRB and ETC FAQ 8.4. If the unit
is being
shot from the flank or the rear, treat the corresponding file or
the last rank, respectively, as the front rank

Majority of unit's footprint is in Ruins

Major
